Voluntary Benefits

Critical illness insurance for your employees.

A serious diagnosis brings costs a health plan was never built to cover, the travel, the childcare, the weeks of lost income. Critical illness insurance pays a lump sum of cash the moment it's diagnosed, so your employee can focus on getting better. For most Minnesota employers it adds at little or no cost.

How critical illness insurance works

Critical illness insurance is voluntary coverage that pays a lump-sum cash benefit when an employee is diagnosed with a covered condition, such as a heart attack, stroke, or cancer. The payout goes straight to the employee, on top of their medical coverage.

A major diagnosis hits a household on more than one front. There's the medical bill, and then there's everything around it, the drive to treatment, time away from work, the help at home. Critical illness insurance pays a fixed amount of cash on diagnosis, and the employee decides how to use it.

We set these plans up through Aflac and other carriers, almost always as a voluntary benefit paid through payroll, so you can offer it at little or no direct cost to the business while giving your team a cushion when it matters most.

What critical illness covers

Lump-sum on diagnosis

A cash benefit paid when a covered condition is diagnosed, including heart attack, stroke, and cancer, among others.

Spend it on anything

Copays, childcare, travel to treatment, or simply the mortgage while someone is out of work.

Stacks on your medical

It pays on top of the health plan, filling the gaps a deductible and out-of-pocket max leave open.

Voluntary and payroll deducted

Employees enroll and pay through payroll, so the company can add it at little or no cost.

Who it's for

Any Minnesota employer who wants to strengthen the package without adding payroll cost. It fits especially well for teams on a higher-deductible medical plan, where a serious illness could mean a large out-of-pocket hit before insurance fully kicks in.

How it works

  1. We review your medical planWe find the gaps a serious diagnosis would expose, the deductible and the out-of-pocket max.
  2. We pick the right planWe match covered conditions and benefit amounts to what would actually help your team.
  3. We set up enrollmentWe add it to your online system so employees can sign up on their own time.
  4. We educate the teamWe explain how and when it pays, because the value is in understanding it before it's needed.
  5. We support claimsIf a diagnosis comes, we help your employee file and get the benefit quickly.

Critical illness coverage pairs naturally with accident insurance, hospital indemnity, and group life, so your team is covered for the gaps a medical plan tends to miss.

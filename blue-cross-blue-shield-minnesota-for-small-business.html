Carriers We Quote

Blue Cross Blue Shield of Minnesota for small business.

Blue Cross Blue Shield of Minnesota is one of the names most employers already know, and for good reason. The question isn't whether it's a real option, it's whether it's the right one for your team and your budget. As an independent broker, we quote Blue Cross alongside the other major carriers so you can actually compare.

What Blue Cross brings to a Minnesota group

Blue Cross Blue Shield of Minnesota is the state's oldest health plan and one of its largest, a nonprofit based in Eagan that has covered Minnesotans for decades. For small business group health, its draw is reach: a broad statewide network plus national access through the Blue Card system.

For a lot of Minnesota employers, Blue Cross is the safe, familiar starting point, the carrier their employees recognize and their doctors already take. That recognition has real value, especially for a team spread across the state or one with people who travel.

We're independent, so we're not here to sell you Blue Cross or steer you away from it. We pull a real Blue Cross quote, set it next to HealthPartners, Medica, UnitedHealthcare, and the regional carriers, and show you where it wins and where another carrier might fit your group better.

Where Blue Cross tends to fit

Broad network reach

A wide statewide network and national access through Blue Card, which helps teams that are spread out or travel for work.

Name employees know

Recognition matters at enrollment. People trust a card they've seen before, which can smooth the rollout.

A range of plan designs

From traditional copay plans to high deductible options that pair with an HSA, there's usually a structure that fits.

Compared, not assumed

We quote Blue Cross next to the other major carriers so the choice is based on real numbers, not on who you've heard of.

Who we work with

Minnesota employers from a couple of employees up to around two hundred who want Blue Cross on the table without locking themselves into it before they've seen the alternatives. If you're renewing a Blue Cross plan that keeps climbing, that's a good moment for a second look too.

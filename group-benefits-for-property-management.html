Benefits by Industry

Group benefits for property management.

Property management teams are split: office and leasing staff on one side, maintenance and field crews on the other, often spread across several properties. A benefits plan has to work for both kinds of work at once. We help Minnesota property managers build coverage that fits the whole team.

What property managers need from a benefits plan

You've got desk-based office staff and hands-on maintenance crews, sometimes across multiple sites. The plan has to serve both, reach people who aren't at a computer, and account for the physical side of the maintenance role, all on a budget that varies with the portfolio.

The challenge in property management is the split workforce. Office staff and maintenance crews have different needs and different risk, and a one-size plan often serves one well and the other poorly. We design with both in mind.

We're independent, so we shop Blue Cross Blue Shield of Minnesota, HealthPartners, Medica, UnitedHealthcare, and the regional carriers, then build a package that works across the whole team.

What we focus on for property managers

Coverage for a split team

A plan that fits office and leasing staff and the maintenance crew, including accident and supplemental coverage with the on-the-job scope checked for the hands-on roles.

Enrollment across sites

We reach staff who are spread across properties and not always at a desk, so everyone gets enrolled.

Flexible cost structure

Funding options and voluntary lines that flex as your headcount and portfolio change.

Year-round advocacy

Your team calls us when a claim gets stuck or someone needs an ID card. We pick up.

Who we work with

Minnesota property management companies from a couple of employees up to around two hundred, residential or commercial, single-site or across a portfolio. Whether you're setting up benefits for the first time or replacing a plan that fits only half your team, we can help.

How it works

  1. We learn your businessThe office and maintenance split, the sites, your budget, and where the current plan falls short.
  2. We shop the marketWe take your census to every major carrier and pull back real quotes with a few plan designs.
  3. We bring you a short listOptions we'd actually recommend, with the trade-offs spelled out for both sides of your team.
  4. We handle the rolloutCarrier paperwork, enrollment across your sites, and the team meetings.
  5. We stay onClaims questions, coverage issues, and next year's renewal, which we start months ahead.

Beyond medical, we round out the package with dental, vision, life and disability, and supplemental coverage through Aflac, so your whole team is covered for what a health plan alone tends to miss.

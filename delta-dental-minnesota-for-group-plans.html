Carriers We Quote

Delta Dental of Minnesota for group plans.

When employees ask whether their dentist is covered, Delta Dental is the name that usually clears the room. Its network is the reason. As an independent broker, we quote Delta Dental against the other dental carriers so you get the network without overpaying for it.

What Delta Dental brings to a group plan

Delta Dental is the most widely recognized dental name in the country, with one of the largest dentist networks anywhere. Delta Dental of Minnesota serves employers across the state, and its biggest draw is simple: the odds that your employees can keep their current dentist are high.

Dental coverage lives or dies on the network. A plan is only worth what it pays at the chair, and the dentist your employees already trust is the one they want to keep going to. Delta Dental's network is broad enough that, for most teams, people don't have to switch.

We're independent, so we quote Delta Dental the way we quote any carrier, on the merits. We set it next to the other dental options on network size, premium, and plan design, then help you decide whether the recognition and reach are worth it for your group.

Where Delta Dental tends to fit

Large dentist network

One of the widest networks in the country, so most employees can keep the dentist they already see.

A name employees trust

Recognition smooths enrollment. People feel comfortable with a plan they already know.

Preventive focus

Cleanings and exams are typically covered up front, with basic and major work layered on top.

Compared, not assumed

We line Delta Dental up against the other dental carriers so the choice is based on real numbers.

Who we work with

Minnesota employers from a couple of employees up to around two hundred who want dental their team will actually use. If keeping current dentists is a priority for your people, Delta Dental is usually worth a quote.

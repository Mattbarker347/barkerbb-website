Everyday Coverage

Dental & Vision people actually use.

Group dental and vision are the benefits your team notices every year — the cleanings, the checkups, the new frames. And because they're affordable, they're an easy win for your benefits package.

Small add, big impact

Compared to medical, dental and vision are inexpensive — but they're the benefits employees use most consistently. Two cleanings a year, an eye exam, a pair of glasses. Families feel the value every time they walk into the dentist's office.

We build dental and vision into your benefits package where it makes sense, whether that's fully employer-paid, voluntary, or a contribution split. You pick the structure that matches your budget; we handle the rest.

What's included

Dental coverage

Preventive, basic, and major services. PPO and HMO networks. Orthodontia add-ons for families.

Vision coverage

Annual exams, frames, lenses, and contacts. LASIK discounts through participating networks.

Flexible funding

Employer-paid, voluntary, or split contribution — whatever fits your budget and strategy.

National networks

In-network providers everywhere, so your team has options regardless of where they live.

Who it's for

Any employer who wants to round out a benefits package with coverage employees will actually use. Dental and vision are often the quickest, simplest wins in a benefits strategy — modest cost, consistent appreciation.

How we make it work

  1. Understand your goalsWe talk through what you want dental and vision to do — attract talent, retain your team, round out an existing plan.
  2. Shop the marketWe pull quotes across multiple carriers and compare network size, premium, and plan design.
  3. Right-size the planWe recommend a structure that fits your budget, whether that's employer-paid, voluntary, or somewhere in between.
  4. Enrollment & setupWe add it to the online enrollment platform, brief your team, and coordinate with the carrier.
  5. Ongoing supportEmployees call us when they need a new ID card or have a question about what's covered.

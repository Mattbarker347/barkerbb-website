Funding, Compared

Fully insured vs level funded medical plans.

Two ways to fund a group medical plan, and the difference comes down to who carries the risk and how much your costs can move. Here's how each one works so you can pick what's aligned with your group.

The real difference is who carries the risk

With a fully insured plan you pay a fixed premium and the carrier carries the risk. With a level funded plan you pay a steady monthly amount but take on some of the risk yourself, with protection built in, and you can share in the savings if your group runs healthy.

When we model medical plans for a Minnesota small business, this is one of the bigger forks. Neither one is better in the abstract. It depends on your group, your appetite for a little variability, and how healthy your claims tend to run.

How a fully insured plan works

You pay a set premium to the carrier, and that's your cost for the year no matter what your employees' claims look like. The carrier takes on all the risk. It's simple and predictable, and it tends to fit groups that want zero surprises and don't want to think about claims data. The trade-off is that if your group is healthy, you don't get any of that savings back. It stays with the carrier.

How a level funded plan works

You pay a steady, level monthly amount that covers an expected claims fund, stop-loss protection, and administration. If your group's claims come in low, you can get a portion back at the end of the year. If they run high, the stop-loss coverage caps your exposure so you're protected. It tends to fit healthier small groups that want more transparency into their claims and a shot at sharing in the savings.

How to choose between them

Think about two things: how much month-to-month predictability you want, and how healthy your group tends to be. If you want it simple and fixed, fully insured leans your way. If your team is relatively healthy and you'd like more insight and a chance at a refund, level funded is worth a hard look. The only way to really know is to run both.

That's what we do. We shop fully insured and level funded options across every major Minnesota carrier, model the real numbers side by side, and help you land on the structure that's aligned with your budget and your team.

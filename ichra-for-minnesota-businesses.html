A Flexible Alternative to Group Health

ICHRA for Minnesota businesses.

If your group renewal keeps climbing and you want more control over the budget, an ICHRA might be the answer. It lets you give your team tax-free money for health coverage instead of running a traditional group plan, and we set the whole thing up for you.

What an ICHRA actually is

An ICHRA, short for Individual Coverage Health Reimbursement Arrangement, lets an employer of any size reimburse employees tax-free for individual health insurance and medical costs, rather than buying one group plan for everyone.

Instead of picking a single plan and hoping it works for your whole team, you set a monthly dollar amount, your employees choose their own individual coverage, and you reimburse them tax-free up to that amount. You control the budget, and your people get to pick a plan that's aligned with what they actually need.

It was created by federal rules in 2020, it works for businesses of any size, and it has become a real option for Minnesota employers who are tired of surprise group renewals.

What an ICHRA gives you

Budget control

You set the monthly allowance, so your benefits cost is predictable instead of swinging with a group renewal.

Employee choice

Your team picks individual plans that fit them, instead of being locked into one group plan.

Class flexibility

You can offer different amounts to different groups of employees, like full-time, part-time, or by location, within the rules.

We run it for you

We set up the ICHRA, handle the administration and reimbursement side, and help your employees enroll in the right individual plan.

Who it fits

ICHRA tends to fit Minnesota employers who want predictable costs, who have a team spread across different needs or locations, or who keep getting group renewals that don't make sense anymore. It is not the right answer for everyone, and that's the honest part. Sometimes a traditional group plan still wins. We model both so you can see the real comparison instead of guessing.

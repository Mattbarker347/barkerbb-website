Everyday Coverage for Minnesota Teams

Group dental insurance for Minnesota businesses.

Dental is the benefit your team notices every year, the cleanings, the fillings, the kid who needs braces. It costs a fraction of medical, and it's one of the simplest wins in a benefits package. We help Minnesota employers add it in a way that fits the budget.

How group dental insurance works in Minnesota

Group dental insurance is employer-offered coverage for preventive, basic, and major dental care. Compared to medical it's inexpensive, and it's the benefit employees use most consistently, which is why it pulls real weight in hiring and retention.

Most dental plans are built in three tiers. Preventive care like cleanings and exams is usually covered in full, basic work like fillings is covered at a share, and major work like crowns and root canals sits at a lower share with an annual maximum. Orthodontia for kids and adults is an add-on when families need it.

We shop dental across multiple carriers, including networks like Delta Dental and Beam, so you can compare premium, network size, and plan design instead of taking the first option a medical carrier bundles in. You pick the structure that fits your budget, and we handle the rest.

What a Minnesota dental plan covers

Preventive, basic, and major

Cleanings and exams, fillings, and the bigger work like crowns and root canals, with PPO and HMO network options.

Orthodontia options

Coverage for braces and aligners that families actually ask about, available as an add-on when it makes sense.

Flexible funding

Fully employer-paid, voluntary, or a contribution split, so the plan matches your budget and your strategy.

National networks

In-network dentists across the state and the country, so your team has options wherever they live.

File a claim or ask a question.

Need help with coverage, a claim, or have a question about your benefits? We're here to help — just fill out the form below and we'll get back to you quickly.

Submit a Claim

Select your claim type, fill in your info, and attach any supporting documents. We'll get it to Aflac on your behalf.

① Select Claim Type
Accident Claim — Documents You May Need
  • ER report or urgent care report
  • Physician's office notes
  • Operative/surgical report (if surgery)
  • Scan/imaging report (if diagnostic imaging)
  • HCFA 1500 or itemized hospital bill (IHB/UB04)
  • Ambulance bill or police report (if applicable)
  • Length of laceration or burn area measurements (if applicable)
  • Physical therapy records (if applicable)
Hospital Indemnity Claim — Documents You May Need
  • Itemized hospital bill (IHB/UB04) with admission and discharge dates/times
  • ER report (if emergency room visit)
  • Operative/surgical report with procedure type or code
  • Physician office notes or medical records
  • Discharge summary
  • Ambulance bill (if applicable)
  • Scan/image report (if diagnostic imaging)
  • Delivery type indicated (if pregnancy-related)
Short-Term Disability Claim — Documents You May Need
  • Physician's statement completed in its entirety
  • Employer's statement completed in its entirety
  • Hospital confinement records (IHB or UB04)
  • Prior year's tax records (if self-employed or policy less than 2 years old)
  • For pregnancy: hospital discharge summary indicating delivery date and type
  • Physician office notes (if disability placed before delivery)
Critical Care Claim — Documents You May Need
  • Discharge summary or medical records with diagnosis
  • Operative/surgical report (if surgery)
  • Ambulance bill or HCFA 1500 (if ambulance used)
  • Itemized lodging billing (if lodging benefit)
  • MapQuest or address proof for transportation benefit
  • Office notes or billing for recovery benefit
Cancer Claim — Documents You May Need
  • Positive pathology report (required for all skin cancer and initial internal cancer claims)
  • MRI, CT scan, or PET scan report (if pathological diagnosis not possible)
  • Itemized hospital bill (IHB/UB04) with admission/discharge dates and times
  • Discharge summary including room type and level of care
  • IHB/UB04/HCFA-1500 for radiation, chemo, or immunotherapy
  • Pharmacy statement/receipts for oral chemo or self-injected medication
  • Operative/surgical report and pathology report (if surgery)
  • Proof of mileage and lodging receipts (if transportation/lodging benefit)

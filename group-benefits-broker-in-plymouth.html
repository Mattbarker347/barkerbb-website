Serving Plymouth Employers

A local group benefits broker for Plymouth businesses.

Plymouth is full of growing companies, office parks, professional firms, and light industrial shops, and for a lot of them benefits have quietly become a second job. That's the part we take off your plate. We're a family-run agency in the Twin Cities, and we help west metro employers build coverage that's aligned with the budget and the team.

Group benefits help built around Plymouth employers

Barker Brothers Benefits works with Plymouth small businesses to shop every major Minnesota carrier, model the real options side by side, and land on a plan that fits where the company is now and where it's heading.

Health coverage costs keep climbing, and sorting it out alone takes time most owners don't have. That's where we come in. We're independent, so we're not tied to a single insurance company, and we quote across Blue Cross Blue Shield of Minnesota, HealthPartners, Medica, UnitedHealthcare, and the regional carriers so you can compare real numbers instead of guessing.

We don't hand you a plan and disappear. We sit on the same side of the table, weigh the options together, and help you make a call that holds up for the long haul.

What we handle for Plymouth employers

Multi-carrier quoting

Real quotes from every major Minnesota carrier, modeled side by side so the trade-offs are clear instead of buried in a PDF.

Plan design guidance

We help you set network, deductible, and employer contribution, and figure out whether an ICHRA, an HSA, or a cooperative option like Health Access Solutions fits.

Enrollment support

We run the enrollment meeting, sit with your employees, and handle the carrier paperwork.

Year-round advocacy

Your team calls us when a claim gets stuck or someone needs an ID card. We pick up.

Who we work with

Plymouth and west metro employers from a couple of employees up to around two hundred, from professional service firms near the 494 corridor to the trades and light manufacturers that keep the area running. Whether you're building a first plan or your renewal just came back with an increase that made you wince, both are good reasons for a second set of eyes.

How it works

  1. We learn your businessSize, budget, what's working, and what your people actually use.
  2. We shop the marketWe take your census to every major carrier and pull back real quotes with a few plan designs.
  3. We bring you a short listTwo or three options we'd actually recommend, in plain English, with the trade-offs spelled out.
  4. We handle the rolloutCarrier paperwork, the enrollment system, and the meeting with your team.
  5. We stay onClaims questions, coverage issues, and next year's renewal, which we start months ahead.

Beyond medical, we round out the package with dental, vision, life and disability, and supplemental coverage through Aflac, so your team is covered for what a health plan alone tends to miss.

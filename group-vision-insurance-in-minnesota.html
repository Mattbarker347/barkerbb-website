Everyday Coverage for Minnesota Teams

Group vision insurance for Minnesota businesses.

Vision is one of the lowest-cost benefits you can offer, and it's one your team uses without thinking, the yearly eye exam, the new glasses, the box of contacts. For a few dollars a month it rounds out a package and your people feel it. We help Minnesota employers add it the easy way.

What group vision insurance covers in Minnesota

Group vision insurance is employer-offered coverage for routine eye care, an annual exam, an allowance toward frames and lenses or contacts, and discounts on extras like LASIK. It's inexpensive, easy to administer, and consistently appreciated.

A vision plan is built around a yearly cadence. It covers a routine eye exam, gives an allowance toward frames and lenses or a supply of contacts, and adds discounts on things like a second pair or LASIK. Because the cost is so low, it's often the benefit that gives a package its finished feel.

We shop vision across multiple carriers and national networks so your team can use a provider close to home or work. You decide how to fund it, fully employer-paid or voluntary, and we build it into the same enrollment system as the rest of your benefits.

What a Minnesota vision plan covers

Annual eye exams

A routine exam each year, the visit that catches more than just a prescription change.

Frames, lenses, and contacts

An allowance toward glasses or a contact supply, with options that fit different prescriptions and styles.

LASIK discounts

Reduced pricing on corrective surgery through participating networks, a perk employees notice.

Flexible funding

Employer-paid or voluntary, so you can offer it at little or no cost to the company.

Who we work with

Any Minnesota employer who wants an easy, affordable add to the benefits package. Vision is frequently a voluntary line, which means your team gets group pricing while the cost to the company stays small, a simple win whether you're starting fresh or filling a gap.

Benefits by Industry

Group benefits for professional services firms.

In a professional services firm, your people are the product. Losing a senior associate or a seasoned accountant is expensive, and a strong benefits plan is one of the quieter reasons good people stay. We help Minnesota firms build coverage that holds onto the talent they've worked hard to develop.

What professional services firms need from a benefits plan

Law firms, accounting practices, consultancies, and agencies tend to run lean on headcount but rich on skill. The plan has to keep experienced people happy, work for both partners and staff, and run smoothly so it never becomes a drain on whoever wears the HR hat.

A professional services firm lives and dies on retention. The cost of replacing a skilled employee, the recruiting, the ramp time, the lost client continuity, dwarfs the cost of a solid benefits plan. Benefits are part of how you keep the bench you've built.

We're independent, so we shop Blue Cross Blue Shield of Minnesota, HealthPartners, Medica, UnitedHealthcare, and the regional carriers, and build a package that feels worth staying for while staying sensible on cost.

What we focus on for professional firms

Plans that retain talent

Coverage that compares well to what a competing firm would offer, so benefits help keep your experienced people in their seats.

Options for partners and staff

Structures that work for owners and employees alike, including richer choices where they make sense.

A rounded package

Medical paired with dental, vision, life, and disability, the lines skilled professionals expect to see.

Service that saves you time

Year-round support so the person managing benefits at your firm isn't fielding carrier calls on top of their real job.

Who we work with

Minnesota professional services firms from a couple of employees up to around two hundred, including law and accounting practices, consultancies, and agencies, whether you're setting up a first plan or upgrading one that no longer reflects the caliber of your team.

How it works

  1. We learn your firmSize, structure, budget, and what you need benefits to do for retention.
  2. We shop the marketWe take your census to every major carrier and pull back real quotes with a few plan designs.
  3. We bring you a short listTwo or three options we'd actually recommend, in plain English, with the trade-offs spelled out.
  4. We handle the rolloutCarrier paperwork, the enrollment system, and the meeting with your team.
  5. We stay onClaims questions, coverage issues, and next year's renewal, which we start months ahead.

Beyond medical, we round out the package with dental, vision, life and disability, and supplemental coverage through Aflac, so the offer reflects the people it's built for.

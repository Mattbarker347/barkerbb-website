Benefits by Industry

Group benefits for healthcare practices.

In a medical, dental, or specialty practice, your clinical staff are skilled, in demand, and expensive to replace. A strong benefits plan is one of the quieter reasons they stay. We help Minnesota practices build coverage that holds onto the team they've trained.

What a healthcare practice needs from a benefits plan

Practices tend to run lean on headcount but high on skill, with clinical roles that are hard to fill. The plan has to keep experienced people happy, work for both owners and staff, and run smoothly so it isn't a drain on whoever manages the office.

A practice lives on retention. Replacing a skilled hygienist, nurse, or tech means recruiting, ramp time, and disruption to patient care, all of which cost far more than a solid benefits plan. Benefits are part of how you keep the team you've built.

We're independent, so we shop Blue Cross Blue Shield of Minnesota, HealthPartners, Medica, UnitedHealthcare, and the regional carriers, and build a package that feels worth staying for while staying sensible on cost.

What we focus on for practices

Plans that retain clinical talent

Coverage that compares well to what another practice or hospital would offer, so benefits help keep your people in their seats.

A rounded package

Medical paired with dental, vision, life, and disability, the lines clinical staff expect to see.

Options for owners and staff

Structures that work for the practice owners and the team alike, including richer choices where they fit.

Service that saves time

Year-round support so your office manager isn't fielding carrier calls between patients.

Who we work with

Minnesota healthcare practices from a couple of employees up to around two hundred, including medical clinics, dental offices, specialty and therapy practices. Whether you're setting up a first plan or upgrading one that no longer reflects your team, we can help.

How it works

  1. We learn your practiceSize, roles, budget, and what you need benefits to do for retention.
  2. We shop the marketWe take your census to every major carrier and pull back real quotes with a few plan designs.
  3. We bring you a short listTwo or three options we'd actually recommend, in plain English, with the trade-offs spelled out.
  4. We handle the rolloutCarrier paperwork, the enrollment system, and the meeting with your team.
  5. We stay onClaims questions, coverage issues, and next year's renewal, which we start months ahead.

Beyond medical, we round out the package with dental, vision, life and disability, and supplemental coverage through Aflac, so the offer reflects the people it's built for.

Core Coverage

Group Health Insurance built around your business.

The anchor of any benefits package — and the one decision that deserves the most attention. We shop every major Minnesota carrier and help you land on the plan that fits your budget and your team.

Coverage from every major MN carrier

When you work with Barker Brothers Benefits you're not locked into one insurance company. We quote across Blue Cross Blue Shield of Minnesota, HealthPartners, Medica, UnitedHealthcare, and the regional players so you can compare real numbers side by side and pick what actually fits.

Group health can feel like a maze. Fully-insured versus level-funded. HMO versus PPO. HSA-compatible versus traditional copay plans. Our job is to translate all of it into plain English and bring you two or three options that make sense for where your business is today and where it's heading.

What's included

Multi-carrier quoting

Real quotes from every major Minnesota carrier, not just one. Side-by-side plan modeling so you can see the trade-offs clearly.

Plan design guidance

We help you pick network type, deductible, employer contribution, and whether to layer in an HRA or HSA.

Enrollment support

We run the open enrollment meeting, help employees pick the right plan, and handle paperwork.

Year-round advocacy

Your team calls us — not HR — when they hit a claims problem, need an ID card, or have a question about coverage.

Who it's for

Minnesota employers from around two employees up to 100+ who want to offer real, meaningful health coverage without losing days of their life on the research. Whether you're setting up group health for the very first time or you've had a plan in place for years and your renewal just landed with a 20% increase, we can help.

How we make it work

  1. Discovery conversationWe sit down and learn your business — size, budget, goals, what's working, what isn't, what your employees actually need.
  2. Market shopWe take your census to every major carrier and pull back real quotes with multiple plan designs.
  3. Recommendations in plain EnglishWe bring you two or three options we'd actually recommend, explain the trade-offs, and help you pick.
  4. ImplementationWe handle carrier paperwork, build your plan in the enrollment system, and run the open enrollment meeting for your team.
  5. Ongoing partnershipWe stay on as your team's point of contact for claims questions, coverage issues, and next year's renewal — which we start working on months ahead of time.

Coverage for Minnesota Employers

Group medical insurance for Minnesota businesses.

For most Minnesota employers, group medical is the biggest line on the benefits budget and the one your team cares about most. It's also the part that quietly turns into a second job. That's the piece we take off your plate. We shop it, compare it, and set it up so you can get back to running the business.

What group medical insurance covers in Minnesota

Group medical insurance is the health plan an employer offers its team, covering doctor visits, hospital stays, prescriptions, and preventive care. In Minnesota it's the anchor of any benefits package and one of the clearest ways to keep good people.

Group medical is the coverage that pays for the everyday and the unexpected, from a yearly checkup to a surgery. A plan that's built well around your budget and your team is one of the strongest tools a small business has for hiring and holding onto people, and sorting through it alone is genuinely hard.

We're independent, so we're not tied to one insurance company. We quote across Blue Cross Blue Shield of Minnesota, HealthPartners, Medica, UnitedHealthcare, and the regional carriers, then model the real options side by side so you can compare actual numbers instead of guessing.

Who we work with

Minnesota employers from a couple of employees up to around two hundred, whether you're setting up group medical for the first time or you've had a plan for years and your renewal just came back with an increase that made you wince. Both are good reasons to get a second set of eyes on it.

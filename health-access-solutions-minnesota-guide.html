Options Worth Knowing

A guide to Health Access Solutions for Minnesota employers.

Health costs keep climbing, and more Minnesota employers are asking whether there's a more affordable path than another group renewal. Health Access Solutions is one of those paths. Here's how it works, where it fits, and the honest tradeoffs, including the parts that aren't for everyone.

What Health Access Solutions is

Health Access Solutions is a reimbursement-based healthcare solution, a defined-contribution model that works as an alternative to a traditional group medical plan. It's built to bring down the total cost of covering a team. It is not ACA-compliant on its own, so it pairs with an individual-coverage path for anyone who needs traditional insurance.

We bring this up when an employer is feeling squeezed by rising premiums and is open to a different structure than a standard group plan. It isn't the right answer for everyone, and we're upfront about that. The point is to put a real, more affordable option on the table next to the traditional route.

How the reimbursement works

It comes in three options based on a per-incident deductible: $1,000, $2,500, or $4,000. For a given incident, the member covers the deductible and Health Access Solutions reimburses the rest of that incident. There's a cap of two incidents per year, so if a third incident happens in the same year, it's fully covered with no deductible. (Plan details govern; we walk you through the specifics for your group.)

The honest tradeoff

Health Access Solutions typically wins on total cost, the ongoing amount you spend to cover the team. The tradeoff is that it isn't traditional ACA insurance, and it carries a waiting period on preexisting conditions. For a lot of groups the savings are worth it, and for some the traditional plan is still the better call. The only way to know is to run the comparison.

Preexisting conditions and the ACA

Because it isn't ACA-compliant and has a preexisting waiting period, we don't leave anyone stranded. We pair it with an individual-coverage path, an ICHRA or an individual plan, so employees who need traditional coverage or who have preexisting conditions still have solid options. People get a route either way.

How we help

We model Health Access Solutions against a traditional group medical plan on the numbers that count, total cost set next to the coverage details, so you can see the real tradeoff instead of a sales pitch. If it's a fit, we set it up and build the companion individual coverage where it's needed. If it isn't, we tell you that too.

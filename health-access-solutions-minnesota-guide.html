Options Worth Knowing

A guide to Health Access Solutions for Minnesota employers.

Health costs keep climbing, and more Minnesota employers are asking whether there's a more affordable path than another group renewal. Health Access Solutions is one of those paths. Here's what it is, where it fits, and the honest tradeoffs.

What Health Access Solutions is

Health Access Solutions is a cooperative healthcare model that works as an alternative to a traditional group medical plan. It's built around bringing down the total cost of covering a team, and for the right group it can be a strong fit. It works differently from standard insurance, so it pays to understand it before you decide.

We bring this up when an employer is feeling squeezed by rising premiums and is open to looking beyond a standard group plan. It isn't the right answer for everyone, and we're upfront about that. The point is to put a real, more affordable option on the table next to the traditional route.

Where it tends to fit

Health Access Solutions tends to fit employers focused on the total cost of coverage, and teams that have watched their group premiums climb year after year without a clear payoff. It's available for Minnesota employers, and there's an individual path too.

The honest tradeoff

Here's the part we lead with, because it's the part that matters. Health Access Solutions typically wins on total cost, the ongoing amount you spend to cover the team. The tradeoff is on the maximum out-of-pocket side, which works differently than a traditional plan. For a lot of groups the cost savings are worth it, and for some the traditional plan is still the better call. The only way to know is to run the comparison.

How we help

We model Health Access Solutions against a traditional group medical plan on the numbers that count, total cost set next to maximum out-of-pocket, so you can see the real tradeoff instead of a sales pitch. If it's a fit, we set it up and stay on to support it. If it isn't, we tell you that too.

That honest comparison is the whole point. You get a clear look at a more affordable option, and a straight answer on whether it actually serves your team.

Carriers We Quote

Beam dental for small business.

Beam is the newer name in dental, built around a simpler, more digital experience than the legacy carriers. For a lot of small groups it comes back sharp on price. Whether it beats the established names for your team is the comparison we run for you.

What Beam brings to a group dental plan

Beam is a dental-focused carrier that has grown quickly with modern, easy-to-administer group dental plans. For small businesses, its appeal is usually a competitive price paired with a clean digital setup, from quoting through enrollment.

Beam took the standard group dental plan and rebuilt the experience around software, which tends to show up as faster quoting, simpler administration, and a member experience that feels current. For a small business owner who doesn't want dental to be a paperwork project, that matters.

We're independent, so we quote Beam on the merits. We set it next to Delta Dental and the other dental carriers on network size, premium, and plan design, then help you weigh the sharper price against the breadth of an established network.

Competitive pricing

Who we work with

Minnesota small businesses that want solid dental without overpaying, and owners who value a clean, low-hassle setup. If price and simplicity are high on your list, Beam is worth pulling a quote on.

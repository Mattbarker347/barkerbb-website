Admin Made Easy

Enrollment & HR Tech that does the work.

No more paper forms. No more chasing signatures. A full online enrollment platform your employees can use from their phone — included with your benefits package.

Modern enrollment, automated back-end

Open enrollment used to mean a stack of paper forms, a clipboard on the break room table, and two weeks of chasing signatures. You get access to a full online enrollment platform instead — so employees sign up in minutes on their phone, and your HR data flows straight to the carriers automatically.

This is the kind of technology that used to only be available to big companies with big budgets. It comes bundled with your benefits package — so your team gets modern tools without you having to hunt for a standalone HR software budget.

What you get

Mobile enrollment

Employees enroll on any device — phone, tablet, laptop. Decision support tools help them pick the right plan.

Carrier integrations

Automated eligibility feeds to major carriers, so new hires and changes flow through without manual re-keying.

Census & reporting

Your employee data lives in one system. Pull it for census, renewals, and compliance whenever you need it.

Year-round access

New hires, life events, and changes happen all year. The platform stays open so your team can self-serve.

Who it's for

Small businesses whose HR person is wearing five other hats and doesn't have time to chase paperwork, and employers who want a modern enrollment experience without buying and configuring standalone HR software.

How we make it work

  1. Build your setupWe load your plans, contribution rates, and eligibility rules into the platform.
  2. Upload your censusEmployees and dependents go into the system so they can log in and enroll.
  3. Run open enrollmentYour team logs in, picks plans, and submits — with side-by-side comparison tools.
  4. Automated carrier feedsApproved enrollments flow to each carrier automatically. No spreadsheets, no manual entry.
  5. Ongoing managementNew hires, terminations, and life events happen year-round, and the platform handles them.

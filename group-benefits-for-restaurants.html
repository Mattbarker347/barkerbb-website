Benefits by Industry

Group benefits for restaurants.

Restaurant benefits run into real-world hurdles: hourly teams, turnover, thin margins, and a mix of full and part-time staff. Done right, benefits help you hold onto good people in an industry where that's half the battle. We help Minnesota restaurants make it work.

What restaurants need from a benefits plan

A restaurant team is mostly hourly, often part-time, and rarely sitting at a desk. Margins are tight and turnover is real. The plan has to be affordable, simple to enroll, and structured so it actually helps you keep your strongest people.

Restaurants face benefits questions most offices never think about. Who counts as full-time. How to offer something meaningful without breaking a thin margin. How to enroll a team that's never at a computer. We build around all of it.

We're independent, so we shop Blue Cross Blue Shield of Minnesota, HealthPartners, Medica, UnitedHealthcare, and the regional carriers, and we lean on low-cost and flexible options where they fit a restaurant's reality.

What we focus on for restaurants

Built for hourly teams

Simple enrollment that works for staff who aren't at a desk, and plan designs that fit how a restaurant is actually staffed.

Cost control on thin margins

Voluntary and supplemental options let you offer real coverage at little or no direct cost to the business.

Help with the full-time count

We help you work through who counts as full-time under the ACA, so you know where your obligations actually sit.

Flexible paths like ICHRA

For a team with variable hours, an ICHRA can let you set a budget and reimburse employees tax-free instead of running a fixed group plan.

Who we work with

Minnesota restaurants, cafes, and hospitality groups from a couple of employees up to around two hundred. Whether you're offering benefits for the first time to compete for staff or rethinking a plan that's too expensive to sustain, we can help you find a fit.

How it works

  1. We learn your businessHeadcount, the full and part-time mix, your budget, and what you want benefits to do for hiring and retention.
  2. We shop the marketWe take your census to every major carrier and pull back real quotes with a few plan designs.
  3. We bring you a short listOptions we'd actually recommend, including voluntary and ICHRA paths, with the trade-offs in plain English.
  4. We handle the rolloutCarrier paperwork, simple enrollment for your team, and the staff meeting.
  5. We stay onClaims questions, new-hire additions, and next year's renewal, which we start months ahead.

Beyond medical, we round out the package with dental, vision, life and disability, and supplemental coverage through Aflac, so your team is covered for what a health plan alone tends to miss.

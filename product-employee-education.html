Ongoing Support

Employee Education that actually lands.

A great benefits package is only valuable if your team understands how to use it. We run enrollment meetings, 1-on-1 sessions, and become the phone number your employees call — not yours.

Benefits your team will actually use

Most employees don't know what's in their benefits package. They signed up during onboarding, clicked through a few screens, and haven't thought about it since. That's a lot of value going unused — and a lot of missed opportunity when something does happen.

We change that. We run group enrollment meetings where we walk through plan options in plain English, then offer 1-on-1 sessions for anyone who wants a deeper look at their specific situation. And then we stay on as their go-to contact all year — so when an employee has a claims question, a billing issue, or a confusing ER bill, they call us instead of interrupting your day.

What's included

Open enrollment meetings

On-site or virtual group meetings where we walk through each plan option in plain language.

1-on-1 plan reviews

Employees can book individual sessions to look at their specific family situation and pick the right plan.

Claims advocacy

When an employee has a billing problem or denied claim, we get on the phone with the carrier and fight for them.

Direct employee hotline

Your team calls us — not you — with questions about their coverage, ID cards, and providers.

Who it's for

Any employer who's ever spent 20 minutes of their day explaining an insurance bill to an employee. Any HR person who fields the same benefits questions every week. Any owner who wants their team to actually value and use the coverage they're paying for.

How we make it work

  1. Kickoff meetingWe run an all-hands session (in person or virtual) where we introduce the benefits package and walk through every option.
  2. 1-on-1 schedulingWe block out time for any employee who wants an individual session to review their specific needs.
  3. Enrollment guidanceWe sit with employees as they enroll online, answering questions as they come up.
  4. Year-round hotlineOnce enrollment is done, we become the point of contact for claims questions, ID cards, and coverage issues.
  5. Mid-year check-insWe touch base with your team so they remember what's available — including HSA contributions, preventive care, and wellness benefits.

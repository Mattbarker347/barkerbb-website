MetLife group benefits for Minnesota businesses.

MetLife is a name your employees already trust, and it's a strong option on the lines that round out a benefits package, life, disability, and the ancillary coverages. As an independent broker, we quote MetLife against the other major carriers so you can see where it fits.

What MetLife brings to a Minnesota group

MetLife is one of the largest and most recognized benefits carriers in the country, with particular strength in group life, disability, and ancillary lines. For a lot of Minnesota employers, it's a dependable choice on the coverages that sit alongside medical.

While medical tends to get the attention, the lines around it, life, disability, and the ancillary coverages, are where a package feels complete. MetLife is a established option there, with the scale and recognition that make enrollment smooth.

We're independent, so we don't lead with any one carrier. We pull a real MetLife quote and set it next to the other major carriers on price, plan design, and service, then show you where it earns a spot in your package.

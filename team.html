Meet the Team

The Barker Brothers.

Two brothers, one mission: make employee benefits simple for Minnesota small businesses.

MB

Matt Barker

Co-Founder & Principal

Matt brings years of experience in employee benefits consulting, specializing in helping small businesses navigate the complexities of health insurance, compliance, and employee engagement.

His focus is on building long-term relationships with clients and delivering personalized service that large firms simply can't match. If you're a BBB client, Matt is likely your primary point of contact — and the person answering your call.

JB

Josh's expertise lies in strategic plan design, carrier negotiations, and supplemental coverage. He's the one who knows which carriers are aggressive this quarter and how to structure a plan that fits.

Our Story

Built on handshakes and phone calls.

We started Barker Brothers Benefits because we saw too many small businesses getting lost in the shuffle at big brokerage firms — treated like small accounts while Fortune 500 clients got the attention.

We wanted to build something different: an agency where the owners actually answer the phone, where employees have a real person to call, and where "benefits admin" stops being a burden on the business owner.

Today, we serve Minnesota companies across industries — from manufacturers to contractors to service businesses — with the same core promise: take the hassle off their plate.

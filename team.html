Meet the Team

The Barker Brothers.

Two brothers, one mission: make employee benefits simple for Minnesota small businesses.

Matt Barker

Co-Owner & Principal

Matt is co-owner of Barker Brothers Benefits and the detail-focused half of the business. He works directly with owners, administrators, and partners to handle the day-to-day logistics of benefits — coordinating communication, integrating technology, and making sure clients always have clear next steps.

Calm and solution-oriented, he’s at his best when things get complicated, known for simplifying complex situations and removing friction for clients. A University of Wisconsin–La Crosse Finance grad raised in the Minneapolis suburbs, Matt is proud to build a business rooted in the community that gave him so much — driven by one goal: making life easier for the people he serves.

Josh Barker

Co-Owner & Principal

Josh is co-owner of Barker Brothers Benefits and the relationship-focused half of the business. He partners with companies, families, and individuals to understand both their professional needs and personal priorities — so no one feels rushed, pressured, or left to navigate complex decisions alone. His day-to-day is client education, hands-on claims support, and being a steady advocate when it matters most.

Before benefits, Josh spent eight years in education, and it still shapes how he works: he believes clarity builds confidence, and he takes pride in breaking down complex topics into clear, practical guidance. Raised in the Minneapolis suburbs, he stays rooted in the community — coaching high school baseball and helping run his local town ball program each summer — and builds every client relationship on trust, mutual respect, and transparency.

Our Story

Founded in 2025.

Barker Brothers Benefits was created with one clear goal: to help small businesses find smarter solutions to rising medical costs while still offering valuable benefits to their employees.

We believe benefits should be understandable, flexible, and financially sustainable. That belief drives everything we do — from how we design benefit strategies to how we educate employees.

As local benefits representatives, we work directly with businesses and their employees, providing hands-on support and in-person guidance when needed. We understand the local market and take pride in being a trusted, accessible partner — not a call center or one-size-fits-all provider.

Aflac group supplemental benefits for Minnesota businesses.

Aflac is the supplemental name almost everyone recognizes, and it's where a lot of Minnesota employers start when they want to round out a benefits package without adding payroll cost. We set it up and service it for your team.

What Aflac actually does for your team

Aflac supplemental coverage pays cash directly to your employees when something happens, an injury, a serious illness, a hospital stay, on top of whatever their medical plan covers. It's usually offered as a voluntary benefit, so employees pay through payroll and the company can add it at little or no cost.

A medical plan handles the doctors and the hospital, but it still leaves a deductible, copays, and all the costs that aren't medical at all, the gas, the time off, the bills at home. Aflac fills that gap by paying cash straight to the employee, who decides how to use it.

Aflac has been the main supplemental line we write since the beginning, and we handle the whole thing: setup, enrollment, and the claims help when an employee actually needs to file.

What Aflac covers

Accident

Cash benefits for injuries, ER visits, fractures, and the follow-up care that comes after.

Critical illness

A lump-sum payout on the diagnosis of a covered condition like heart attack, stroke, or cancer.

Hospital indemnity

A set cash amount for a hospital stay, paid on top of the medical plan.

Cash, paid to the employee

Benefits go straight to your team member to spend however they need, not to a provider.

Who it's for

Any Minnesota employer who wants to strengthen the package without adding payroll cost, and it fits especially well for teams on a higher-deductible medical plan, where a big claim could mean a real out-of-pocket hit. Aflac supplemental and Aflac dental are separate products, so a group can carry one, both, or just the supplemental line.

How we set it up

  1. We review your medical planWe find the gaps a big claim would expose, the deductible and the out-of-pocket max.
  2. We pick the right productsWe recommend the supplemental lines that address the real gaps, not a bundle nobody uses.
  3. We set up enrollmentWe add it to your online system so employees can sign up on their own time.
  4. We educate the teamWe explain how it pays, because the value only shows up when people understand it.
  5. We support claimsWhen something happens, we help your employee file and get paid quickly.

Aflac pairs naturally with group medical, dental, vision, and life, so your team is covered for the things a health plan alone tends to miss.

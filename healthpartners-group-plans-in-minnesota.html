Carriers We Quote

HealthPartners group plans for Minnesota businesses.

HealthPartners is a different kind of carrier. It runs its own clinics and hospitals as well as the insurance, which can mean a smoother experience for employees who use its care system. Whether that integration pays off for your team is the real question, and that's what we help you work out.

What HealthPartners brings to a Minnesota group

HealthPartners is a Bloomington-based, consumer-governed nonprofit and one of the largest integrated health organizations in the country. It both insures members and delivers care through its own clinics and hospitals, which gives it a distinct angle in the Twin Cities market.

The thing that sets HealthPartners apart is integration. Because it owns much of the care your employees would use, the insurance side and the care side talk to each other, and for teams centered in the Twin Cities that can make for a clean, connected experience.

We're independent, so we quote HealthPartners the same way we quote everyone: on the merits. We pull a real quote and set it next to Blue Cross Blue Shield of Minnesota, Medica, UnitedHealthcare, and the regional carriers, then show you where the integrated model helps your group and where it might not.

Minnesota employers from a couple of employees up to around two hundred, especially teams based in the Twin Cities where the HealthPartners care network runs deep. If your people already use its clinics, it's well worth a quote.

Carriers We Quote

Medica group plans for Minnesota businesses.

Medica is a Minnesota carrier through and through, and for a lot of Twin Cities employers it lands as a strong middle ground on network and price. Whether it's the right fit depends on your team, which is exactly what an independent broker is for. We quote Medica next to the other major carriers so you can see for yourself.

What Medica brings to a Minnesota group

Medica is a Minnesota-based nonprofit health plan headquartered in Minnetonka, serving employers across the Upper Midwest. For small business group health, it often shows up as a competitive option on price with a network that covers the Twin Cities and greater Minnesota well.

Medica tends to earn its spot on the short list. It's a local carrier that knows the Minnesota market, and for many groups it comes back with a quote that's hard to ignore once you set it next to the bigger national names.

We're independent, so our job isn't to push Medica, it's to test it. We pull a real Medica quote and put it side by side with Blue Cross Blue Shield of Minnesota, HealthPartners, UnitedHealthcare, and the regional carriers, then show you where it fits and where it doesn't.

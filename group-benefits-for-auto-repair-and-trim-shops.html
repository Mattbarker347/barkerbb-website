Benefits by Industry

Group benefits for auto repair and trim shops.

A good technician is hard to find and worth keeping, and benefits are one of the few things a small shop can offer that a bigger competitor down the road might not. We help Minnesota auto repair and trim shops put together coverage that fits the work and the budget.

What a shop needs from a benefits plan

Shop work is hands-on, around lifts, tools, and real injury risk, and the teams are usually small with tight margins. The plan has to protect a physical crew, help you hold onto skilled techs, and stay affordable for a small business.

For a shop, benefits aren't a corporate formality, they're a hiring tool and a way to take care of the people who keep the bays running. A plan built for an office doesn't fit a crew on the floor, so we build around the real work.

We're independent, so we shop Blue Cross Blue Shield of Minnesota, HealthPartners, Medica, UnitedHealthcare, and the regional carriers, then put together a package that makes sense for a small, hands-on team.

What we focus on for shops

Coverage for a physical crew

Accident and supplemental coverage that pays cash for injuries, with the on-the-job scope checked so a shop team isn't stuck with an off-the-job-only plan.

A tool to keep techs

A real benefits offering gives a skilled technician a reason to stay instead of taking the next offer down the street.

Cost control for a small shop

Funding options and voluntary lines that let you offer meaningful coverage without overextending a small business.

Simple, hands-on setup

We handle the paperwork and run enrollment in person, so it doesn't pull you off the floor.

Who we work with

Minnesota auto repair, body, and trim shops from a couple of employees up to around two hundred, whether you're offering benefits for the first time to compete for techs or replacing a plan that never fit a shop crew.

How it works

  1. We learn your shopCrew size, the work, your budget, and what you want benefits to do for hiring.
  2. We shop the marketWe take your census to every major carrier and pull back real quotes with a few plan designs.
  3. We check the coverage scopeWe match accident and supplemental plans to a hands-on crew, on the job and off.
  4. We handle the rolloutCarrier paperwork, in-person enrollment, and the meeting with your team.
  5. We stay onClaims questions, injury follow-ups, and next year's renewal, which we start months ahead.

Beyond medical, we round out the package with dental, vision, life and disability, and supplemental coverage through Aflac, so your crew is covered for what a health plan alone tends to miss.
